About Me

Emily Adeyanju is currently the executive writer of the New York State Insurance Fund. She previously served as the manager of communications at the 1199SEIU Child Care Funds, where she oversaw the Funds' communication efforts, ensuring accuracy and consistency in the delivery of its message, and in the effective conveyance of its vision to the public. She also worked to secure private, corporate and government funds to support critical program expansion efforts, precipitate the development of new programs, and promote the longevity of the Fund.

Ms. Adeyanju's previous positions involve writing, communications, creative content development for websites, presentations, editing, and consulting work in graphic design as well as in video editing. Ms. Adeyanju graduated with honors from Pratt Institute with a Master of Fine Arts in Computer Graphics and Interactive Media; she received her undergraduate degree from Wesleyan University.
